Pages Resource
Explore the most important pages for your website. Use these as a starting point for your own site, following best practices from top website builders and modern web design.
  • Home — The main landing page. Should be clear, welcoming, and guide users to key areas.
  • About — Tell your story, mission, and what makes your brand unique.
  • Contact — Make it easy for visitors to reach you. Include a form, email, phone, and address if relevant.
  • Blog — Share news, updates, and articles to engage your audience and boost SEO.
  • Portfolio — Showcase your work, projects, or case studies with images and descriptions.
  • Services — List and describe what you offer, with clear calls to action.
  • FAQ — Answer common questions to help users and reduce support requests.
  • Testimonials — Build trust with quotes from happy clients or customers.
  • Pricing — Clearly display your plans, packages, or rates.
  • Gallery — Display images, videos, or media in an attractive layout.
  • Shop / Store — Sell products or services online with e-commerce features.
  • Login / Register — Allow users to create accounts and access personalized features.
  • Privacy Policy — Inform users about data collection and privacy practices.
  • Terms & Conditions — Outline the rules and legal terms for using your site.
Best Practices:
• Keep navigation simple and intuitive.
• Use clear headings and calls to action.
• Make sure your site is mobile-friendly and loads quickly.
• Use high-quality images and concise text.
• Include contact info and social links on every page.
• Regularly update your content for freshness and SEO.
